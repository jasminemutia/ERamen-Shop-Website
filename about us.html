About Us

ERamen shop is a historical ramen that originated in Japan back in 1910. Since 2015, ERamen has expanded its operations to Indonesia. What makes ERamen special is its menu use organic flour to make delicious ramen noodles. Customers can experience many variety of ramen and enjoy various kinds of tempura dishes.

Indulge in a bowl of hot ramen and experience the flavors that have captivated us for over a century. Come and treat yourself to the unbeatable delights of ERamen!

Our Vision

Our vision is to provide customers around Indonesia with a genuine taste of Japan's rich culinary heritage, leading online destination for authentic and extraordinary ramen.

We strive to redefine the online ramen experience by offering exceptional quality, convenience, and a true reflection of traditional flavors and techniques.

Our Mission

Authenticity

Our mission is to maintain the authenticity of traditional ramen using old recipes and ingredients.

We try to give a taste of Japanese ramen culture that is adapted to the Indonesian tongue.

Excellence Quality

We are committed to delivering the highest quality ramen to our customers.

Our mission is to provide the finest ingredients to create rich flavor and perfectly cooked noodles.

Convenience and Accessibility

We strive to provide an efficient delivery and carefully packaged ramen that maintains its freshness.

We aim to bring the convenience and joy of enjoying a bowl of ramen to our customer's doorsteps.
